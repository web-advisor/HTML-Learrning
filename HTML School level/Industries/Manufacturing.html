MANUFACTURING INDUSTRIES

Manufacturing is an important sector of any economy. The economic Strength of a country is measured by the development of Manufacturing Industries. Production of goods in large quantities after processing from raw materials to more valuable products is called Manufacturing. People employed in the secondary or manufacturing actitvities manufacture the primary materials into finished goods.


Manufacturing


Factors influencing the location of an Industry are:
  • Cost of obtaining raw materials at site
  • Cost of Production at site
  • Decision to locate Factory at site
  • Cost of distribution of Production
